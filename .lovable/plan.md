

## Pankaj Singh — AI-Powered Digital Marketing Portfolio

### Design Direction
- **Color palette**: Deep purple (#26215C) primary, gold (#D4AF37) accents, soft lavender highlights (#AFA9EC), dark gradient backgrounds
- **Typography**: Playfair Display for headings (serif, luxury feel), DM Sans for body text — matching your resume's elegant style
- **Vibe**: Royal, luxurious, modern — gold shimmer effects, smooth scroll animations, glass-morphism cards

### Pages & Sections (Single-page scroll)

1. **Hero Section**
   - Full-screen dark purple gradient background
   - Your name in large gold serif typography with "AI-Powered Marketing Expert" tagline
   - Interactive 3D rotating globe (React Three Fiber) with glowing connection points — symbolizing global digital reach
   - Subtle floating gold particles in the background
   - CTA button: "Explore My Work" with gold border glow

2. **About Section**
   - Your professional summary from the resume
   - 5+ years experience stat with animated counter
   - Key strengths displayed as elegant gold-bordered pills
   - "AI-Driven" badge with a subtle glow effect

3. **Skills Section**
   - Four skill categories (Paid Media, Organic & Content, Analytics & Tools, Specialisations) displayed in glass-morphism cards
   - Skill pills with color-coded categories matching your resume
   - Hover animations with gold shimmer

4. **Experience Timeline**
   - Vertical timeline with gold accent line
   - Four roles displayed as expandable luxury cards
   - Company names, date chips, and key achievements
   - Smooth reveal animations on scroll

5. **Certifications & Education**
   - Elegant card layout with purple gradient accents
   - Google & Digiperform certifications highlighted

6. **Contact Section**
   - Contact form with glass-morphism styling
   - Phone, email, location info with gold icons
   - Gold gradient CTA button

### 3D & Animations
- React Three Fiber for the interactive 3D globe in the hero
- Scroll-triggered fade-in animations throughout
- Gold particle effects in the background
- Smooth hover transitions on all interactive elements
